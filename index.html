About

Neon Mahjong Solitaire is built by Anulfo Acosta, an indie iOS developer. It's a single-player tile-matching puzzle — not the four-player gambling game of Mahjong.

Free to play with optional rewarded ads for extra hints. Optional in-app purchases for theme packs ($1.99) and ad removal ($3.99 for unlimited hints, no ads ever). See the privacy policy for details.
