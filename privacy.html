Privacy Policy

Effective: April 28, 2026 · Last updated: April 28, 2026

Neon Mahjong Solitaire ("the app", "we") is built with privacy as a default. This page explains, in plain language, what happens to data while you use the app.

Short version: We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app works fully offline. Game Center sign-in and in-app purchases are handled directly by Apple and never touch our servers — because we don't operate any.

What we don't do

  • We don't ask for or collect your name, email, phone number, or location.
  • We don't use third-party analytics. There is no Google Analytics, no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no anything.
  • We don't show ads in this version of the app.
  • We don't track you across other apps or websites.
  • We don't sell, share, or transmit any data to third parties.
  • We don't use cookies. (This is an iOS app; there are no cookies anyway.)

What stays on your device

The following data is created and stored entirely on your device, in iOS's standard UserDefaults. It never leaves your phone or iPad:

  • Game progress — best times per layout, win counts, daily streak, completed daily challenges
  • App preferences — sound on/off, haptics on/off, currently selected theme
  • Purchase status — which theme packs and "Remove Ads" upgrade you've bought (verified against Apple's receipts)

Uninstalling the app removes all of this. You can also reset stats at any time via Settings → Data → Reset Stats.

Apple-handled features

Game Center

If you choose to sign in to Game Center, Apple manages everything: your nickname, your authentication, your friends list, and your leaderboard scores. We never see your Apple ID or personal information. The app submits your best times to per-layout leaderboards hosted by Apple, but those submissions go directly from your device to Apple's servers. We have no servers and we receive no copy.

Game Center is entirely optional. The app is fully playable without signing in.

In-App Purchases

All purchases (theme packs, Remove Ads) are processed by Apple's StoreKit. Apple handles payment, billing, and receipt verification. We never see your credit card, billing address, or App Store account details. The app stores only a verified receipt token from Apple, locally on your device, to confirm you own what you've bought.

Permissions we request

The current version of the app requests no permissions. Specifically:

  • No camera, microphone, photos, contacts, calendar, location, motion, or health data.
  • No push notifications (yet — if added in a future version, this policy will be updated and the app will ask for your permission first).
  • No App Tracking Transparency prompt — we don't track you.

Children's privacy

The app is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children — or, for that matter, anyone. Because we collect no data, COPPA, GDPR-K, and similar children's-privacy regulations are satisfied by default.

Future changes

If a future version of the app introduces ads (planned for v1.2 via Google AdMob), this policy will be updated to fully describe what data those ads use, the App Tracking Transparency prompt, and your options to opt out. We will not add data collection silently.

Material changes to this policy will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact
